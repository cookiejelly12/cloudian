Wordcloud Generator


Language: 한국어 English


How to use:
1. Type the text you want to make into a wordcloud in the box below.
2. Choose the word cloud shape you want from 'Shapes'.
3. Press the 'Generate' button and wait.
4. Download the completed word cloud image.

Note:
1. Words in parentheses may not be recognized properly.
2. If the sentence is too short or the number of words is small, the word cloud may not be generated properly.
3. Some punctuation marks or numbers can be printed together.
